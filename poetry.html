Our Method

Here is a breakdown of the steps and processes we used to create this work.

Step 1: Initial Research

The first step involved collecting preliminary data and reviewing existing literature. This set the foundation for our interactive map.

Step 2: Sound Collection

We then moved into the field to record the audio that you hear in the project. Each sound was carefully edited and mastered.

Step 3: Design & Development

Finally, the visual and interactive elements were built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
